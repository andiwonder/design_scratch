Tracking happiness is actually very simple and
takes less than 5 minutes per day.

The entire method is based on three simple ideas:

1

Determine your daily happiness rating

Rate every single day based on your feeling of happiness, on a scale from 1 to 10.

2

Determine the happiness factors

Which factors influenced your happiness, both positive and negative?

3

Keep it up and learn!

As you continue tracking happiness, your data will become more valuable.
